History

Cassell Guard is a student organization at Virginia Tech for those who have a passion Hokie basketball. Our goal is to boost fan interaction and create a sense of community around Hokie Hoops.

Choose a Topic:

Cassell Coliseum

Cassell Coliseum is the home court of the Virginina Tech Hokies Basketball team. Currently, the team has a record of 11-3.

Virginia Tech

Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, or Virginia Tech, is an American public university loacted in Blacksburg, Virginia.

Roles

Cassell guard consists of two membesrhip options: General Membership and the Executive Board. They organize student giveaways, run the social media account, and plan Cassell Guard events.


Membership Options

General Membership

Every student who sits in the student section on game days is considered to be a general member of Cassell Guard.

Executive Board

The Executive Board consists of die-hard Virginia Tech Basketball fans that are hand-selected each year through an application process.
